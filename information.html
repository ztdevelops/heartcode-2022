What is narcissism?

People with narcissistic personality disorder (NPD) are in love with an idealized, grandiose image of themselves. Theyâ€™re in love with this inflated self-image precisely because it allows them to avoid deep feelings of insecurity.

A narcissistic personality disorder causes problems in many areas of life, such as relationships, work, school or financial affairs. People with narcissistic personality disorder may be generally unhappy and disappointed when they're not given the special favors or admiration they believe they deserve. They may find their relationships unfulfilling, and others may not enjoy being around them.

Differentiating Narcissism and Confidence

Narcissism is often confused with confidence.

Confidence is...

  • Believing in yourself
  • Individuals do not need affirmation
  • Willing to admit fault

Narcissism is...

  • Exaggerated sense of self worth and capabilities
  • Individuals crave affirmation
  • Avoids blame
